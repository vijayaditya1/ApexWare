Privacy Policy

Transparent data governance for our published mobile and web software.

Last Updated: June 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to mobile applications developed and published by ApexWare Apps. While this website is provided for informational purposes and does not intentionally collect personal information beyond what is necessary for its normal operation, our mobile applications may collect certain information to provide services, process purchases, display advertisements, and improve user experience.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use our applications, we may collect:

  • Name (if voluntarily provided)
  • Email address (if you create an account or contact us)
  • Device information (device model, operating system, app version, language)
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Gameplay and usage information
  • Crash reports and diagnostic information

2. In-App Purchases

Our applications may offer optional in-app purchases. Payments are securely processed through the Google Play Store or other authorized payment providers.

We do not collect, store, or process your payment card or banking information.

3. Advertising

Our applications may display advertisements using third-party advertising services such as:

  • Google AdMob
  • Unity Ads

These services may collect advertising identifiers, IP address, approximate location, device information, and usage data to provide personalized or non-personalized advertisements, measure campaign performance, and help prevent fraud.

4. How We Use Information

Information collected may be used to:

  • Provide and improve our applications
  • Deliver game features
  • Restore purchases
  • Display advertisements
  • Analyze performance and fix bugs
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

Information may be shared with trusted third-party service providers only when necessary to operate our applications, process purchases, provide advertising, perform analytics, comply with legal obligations, or improve our services.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no electronic storage or transmission method is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. User Data Deletion Requests

In accordance with Google Play Store User Data policies, users retain the right to request the complete purge of their account and associated telemetry data. To initiate an automated data deletion request, transmit an email to dev@apexware.in containing the subject line "Data Purge Request with your [Insert App Name]".
